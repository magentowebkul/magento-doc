# FAQ

**Is Adornment a standalone theme or a child theme?**
It works with Hyva. Hyva must be installed first.

**Do I need coding skills to build the homepage?**
No. The **Homepage Configurator** and **Header & Footer Builder** are visual admin tools.

**How many homepage sections can I add?**
As many as you like. They show from top to bottom based on **Sort Order**. There are 10 section types to mix and match.

**Can I show different homepages per store view?**
Yes. Use **Store ID** to control where a section appears. Use `0` for all stores.

**Where are mega menu settings saved?**
They are saved from **Content -> Homepage Configurator -> Header & Footer Builder**.

**Can I use my own logo and colors?**
Yes. Set your logo in **Header settings**. Theme colors can be adjusted during setup or customization.

**Does it support swatches and configurable products?**
Yes — configurable products and swatches use the standard Magento + Hyva rendering, styled by the theme.

**Is the checkout included?**
The theme is designed to work with Hyva Checkout when Hyva Checkout is available on your store.

**How do I export a documentation page?**
Use the page-tools drawer (bottom-right) to export to **PDF, Word, Markdown, HTML, RTF, or plain text**, or press <kbd>P</kbd> for the PDF dialog. Search with <kbd>⌘</kbd>/<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd>.

**What are the performance characteristics?**
The storefront is lightweight and designed for strong PageSpeed results.
