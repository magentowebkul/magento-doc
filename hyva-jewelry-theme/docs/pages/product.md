# Product Page

The **Product Page** gives shoppers the full product details before they buy. It shows the image gallery, price, options, stock, quantity, buttons, description, extra information, reviews, and related products.

## Supported product types

The theme supports standard Magento product types:

| Product type | Example use |
| --- | --- |
| **Simple Product** | One product with no choices, such as one ring or one bracelet. |
| **Configurable Product** | A product with choices, such as metal color and ring size. |
| **Bundle Product** | A set where shoppers choose items, such as a bridal jewellery set. |
| **Grouped Product** | A collection of related products sold together or shown together. |
| **Downloadable Product** | A digital item, such as a jewellery care guide PDF. |
| **Virtual Product** | A service, plan, warranty, or digital certificate. |

## What shoppers see

| Area | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Image gallery** | Shows product images. Shoppers can open image zoom. |
| **Mobile image swipe** | Lets mobile shoppers swipe product images. |
| **Product name** | Shows the full product name. |
| **Price** | Shows regular price and sale price when a discount is active. |
| **Short description** | Shows quick product details near the top. |
| **Stock message** | Shows In Stock, Out of Stock, or low stock message. |
| **Product options** | Shows choices like metal color, ring size, bundle items, or downloadable links. |
| **Quantity** | Lets shoppers increase or decrease quantity where the product type needs it. |
| **Add to Cart** | Adds the product to the cart. |
| **Buy Now** | Sends the shopper straight to checkout after adding the item. |
| **Wishlist** | Adds the product to wishlist. |
| **Compare** | Adds the product to compare. |
| **Share** | Lets shoppers share or copy the product link. |
| **Description** | Shows full product details. |
| **Additional Information** | Shows product attributes. |
| **Reviews** | Shows reviews and the review form. |
| **Related Products** | Shows similar products. |
| **Upsell Products** | Shows other products shoppers may like. |

## Product options

Different product types show different options.

| Product type | What to check |
| --- | --- |
| **Configurable** | Check each option, such as metal color and ring size. Price can change after choosing an option. |
| **Bundle** | Check each bundle choice, quantity, summary, and final price. |
| **Grouped** | Check the grouped item list and stock. |
| **Downloadable** | Check the file link or sample link. |
| **Virtual** | Check service details, price, quantity, and Add to Cart. |

## Reviews

The product page includes:

- Rating stars
- Review count
- Reviews tab
- Write a Review form
- Nickname, summary, and review fields

If reviews do not show, check that reviews are enabled in Magento and approved in Admin.

## How to check a product page

1. Open a product from a category page or search page.
2. Check product images and image zoom.
3. Check price, sale price, and stock message.
4. Select all required options.
5. Change the quantity if the page shows a quantity field.
6. Click **Add to Cart**.
7. Test **Buy Now**.
8. Test Wishlist, Compare, and Share.
9. Open Description, Additional Information, and Reviews.
10. Check Related Products and Upsell Products.
11. Repeat this for each product type used in your store.

## Good practice

- Use clear product names.
- Add more than one product image when possible.
- Add jewellery attributes like material, metal color, stone, size, purity, and certification.
- Keep the short description easy to read.
- Add related and upsell products for better shopping flow.
